## 2) Execution Plan by Phase

## Phase A — Editorial Lock (Apr 5–Apr 10)

### Completed
- Restored signature closing lines in opening section.
- Restored three-deliverable structural promise.
- Added bridge into the 30-Day Sprint.
- Added filter-by-filter scored wedges in examples.
- Added explicit 1–5 scoring rubrics per filter.

### Remaining checks
1. Run one final consistency sweep for voice and repetition.
2. Validate chapter ordering and heading hierarchy for export.
3. Confirm all examples follow the same scoring table format.

**Exit Criteria:** Manuscript locked with no open critical editorial defects.

## Phase B — Packaging + Production (Apr 10–Apr 18)

1. Freeze title/subtitle decision:
   - Recommended working package: **Build What Pays: The Six-Filters System for Finding Business Ideas That Actually Work**
2. Finalize cover direction (authority + practicality + ruthless clarity positioning).
3. Build interior style rules (heading scale, table treatment, callout style).
4. Export + QA EPUB and print PDF.
5. Perform final proof pass on devices (Kindle, Apple Books, mobile PDF).

**Exit Criteria:** Distribution-ready assets validated.

## Phase C — Launch Readiness (Apr 12–Apr 22)

1. Publish author landing page with chapter sample and worksheet lead magnet.
2. Prepare 14-day launch email sequence:
   - Pre-launch value emails (framework snippets)
   - Launch day announcement
   - Case study follow-ups
3. Build outbound list for ARCs (creator economy, indie hacker, bootstrapper operators).
4. Create launch content kit (10 short posts + 3 long-form essays + 5 quote cards).

**Exit Criteria:** Marketing pipeline ready before publication date.

---

## 3) Quality Gates (Must Pass)

- **Gate 1: Editorial Quality** — no unresolved structural defects; no broken formatting; examples standardized.
- **Gate 2: Product Quality** — EPUB/PDF rendering clean; links and TOC functional; worksheets readable.
- **Gate 3: Market Quality** — title/subtitle and metadata aligned to target audience search intent.
- **Gate 4: Launch Quality** — prebuilt campaign assets and outreach list ready before release.

---

## 4) Risk Register + Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Title indecision delays packaging | High | Decide by Apr 12 with one fallback option |
| Design iteration drifts timeline | Medium | Timebox to 2 cover rounds |
| Production formatting issues in EPUB | Medium | Device QA pass before final export |
| Launch starts without ARC momentum | High | Build ARC list + outreach scripts one week before launch |
| Scope creep on manuscript changes | High | Editorial lock on Apr 10; post-lock changes require explicit approval |

---

## 6) Definition of Done (Book Shipping)

The book is considered shipped when all conditions are met:
1. Manuscript is editorially locked and versioned.
2. EPUB + print PDF pass QA.
3. Retail metadata finalized.
4. Launch assets and ARC outreach are live.
5. Publication date is publicly announced with buying path.
