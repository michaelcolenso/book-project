# Gumroad Product Package — Build What Pays

## Product Positioning

**Product name:** Build What Pays
**Subtitle:** A Solo Builder’s Field Manual for Finding Ideas That Pay

### Core promise
Stop guessing what to build. Use a repeatable system to find stronger opportunities, kill weak ones fast, and get to proof before you waste months building the wrong thing.

### Ideal buyer
- solo builders
- indie hackers
- technical operators
- AI-native founders
- sharp generalists who can build, but keep choosing weak ideas

### Not for
- readers who want motivational founder content
- beginners looking for coding tutorials
- people who want 100 vague startup ideas with no scoring or validation system

### Positioning sentence
This is a short, tactical playbook for builders who want a better way to choose what to build, using money trails, painful workflows, and public-data gaps instead of trend chasing.

### Main sales copy
Most builders do not have an execution problem.

They have an idea-selection problem.

They follow trends, overvalue compliments, mistake interesting for valuable, and burn months building products that never had enough pain or spending behind them to work.

**Build What Pays** gives you a sharper way to choose.

Inside, you will get a practical system for:
- finding higher-signal opportunities
- spotting proof of spend and proof of pain
- narrowing broad ideas into strong wedges
- killing weak ideas before they eat your time
- running a 30-day validation sprint before full product build

This is built for people who can make things. The problem is not execution. The problem is deciding what deserves execution.

### Why this is different
Most “startup idea” products give you volume instead of judgment.

This one is built around judgment.

You are not getting 100 vague ideas. You are getting a method for finding and pressure-testing better opportunities on your own, plus examples that show what a strong wedge actually looks like.

### Outcome promise
By the end, you should be able to:
- identify stronger opportunities faster
- reject weak opportunities with less hesitation
- narrow big ideas into testable wedges
- decide what deserves the next 30 days of your life
