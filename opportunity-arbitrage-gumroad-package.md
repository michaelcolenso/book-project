## FAQ

### Is this a book of business ideas?
Not really. It includes 15 opportunity cards, but the product is the method, not the list.

### Is this only for AI builders?
No. AI helps, but the framework is about opportunity selection, buyer pain, and validation. It works whether the final product uses AI, automation, data, or plain software.

### Is this beginner-friendly?
Yes, but it is written for builders and operators. It assumes you want directness, not hand-holding.

### Will this help me choose between multiple ideas?
Yes. That is one of the main reasons it exists.

### Why not just give 100 ideas?
Because more ideas usually create more noise. This playbook is built around discrimination, not volume.
